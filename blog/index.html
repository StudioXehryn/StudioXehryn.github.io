Gate of Providence has been out for almost two weeks now! So here is my second blog post. I meant to post something in November but things got hectic around that time, like my hard drive dying on me. Thankfully I run my OS on an SSD, so I still had a computer, but it meant that I couldn't do any development during that time (my programs were on the HDD). I bought a replacement drive (which promptly went on sale for the first time ever AFTER I received it) so it is all good now.
