Dreamshot Dissonance

Bullet Hell / Danmaku

Platform: Windows PC
Released: June 5, 2014

A 16-year old boy named Ayumu is in a dream. This dream, formed by the culminations of the regrets he had harbored in his life, has become something called a Dreamshot Dissonance. He will meet people close to him in this dream, including his classmate, his best friend, and his little sister. Ayumu must face the manifestations of his own regrets and eventually conquer his dark, inner self. Will he triumph over the seven sins of humanity, and topple his pride? Or will he wake up, never to overcome this Dreamshot Dissonance?
