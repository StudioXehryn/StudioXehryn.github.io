Welcome to Studio Xehryn

Studio Xehryn (pronounced Zay-Rin) is a small (one-man) independent video game development studio created by a guy named Henry in 2014. This studio was created as a freelance entity for collaboration with other artists and developers, hence the prominence of the X in the studio name. At the moment, I specialize in creating 2D Role-Playing Games. I have experience with all aspects of game development, with programming and game design being my main background and area of expertise. I have also done work with digital art, story and script writing, music composition, and video editing as well.

On this website, you'll find the games, tutorials, free game assets and resources, and devblogs that have been put out over the years.

If you would like to contact me for collaboration or to commission a game or design consultation, you can send me a private message on Reddit or message me on Twitter. (A Reddit PM will be seen within 24 hours.)
