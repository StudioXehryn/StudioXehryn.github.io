Welcome to Studio Xehryn

Studio Xehryn (pronounced Zay-Rin) is a small (one-man) independent video game development studio run by a guy named Henry. On this website, you'll find our games, tutorials, and dev blog.

At the moment, I specialize in creating Role-Playing Games. I have experience with all aspects of game development, with programming and game design being my main background. I have also done work with art, writing, and music as well.

If you would like to contact me for any reason, you can message me on Twitter or PM me on Reddit.
