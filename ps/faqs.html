Frequently Asked Questions

I would like to stream / do a Let's Play of Pestilence Stirs.

Please feel free to do so. Contact me if you would like to be featured a Media page!
Don't forget to leave a review when you are done playing as well, it really helps!

I think I found a bug/issue!

Contact me on Reddit or Twitter, or report it on the Studio Xehryn subreddit.

How can I make more money to buy things?

You can only get money by curing people. Spend your money wisely - shop around and you might find lower prices!

I'm having trouble with combat!

Don't feel that you need to conserve your Food / Drink and Solutions. You can always buy more during the intermissions. Also remember to use Miracles if you are in tough spot!

Why doesn't William have the "beak" that plague doctors wear?

The iconic plague doctor beak didn't appear until the 17th century, well after the 14th century when the Black Death swept Europe. This game is based off that time period, although in its own world.
